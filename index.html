These interests encompass all aspects of Artificial Neural Networks (unsupervised learning, reinforcement learning, capabilities, alignment, all modalities, science of deep learning, etc.).

I'm currently a PhD student at Mila working mostly with Blake Richards, Irina Rish, and David Krueger. Before I started focusing on the scaling perspective, I mostly worked on out-of-distribution generalization and generalization theory with Yoshua's and Aaron's students.
